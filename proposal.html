Fantasy Football: How Can We Improve the Experience?

Problem Description

Fantasy Football?

Fantasy Football is a point-based game tied to the statistics of real-life football athlethes. You, the owner and manager of your fantasy football team, are in charge of the drafting and management of the team throughout the real-time season.

What's the significance?

As this game is often played competitively, research prior to drafting and throughout the season is essential in improving your chances to win. Your players' performance is a direct representation of their performance in games played and to help with the draft selection or waiver wire pick up, some form of accessible stat tracker would assist in your decision making. In this statistics based game, having access to these statistics would allow the player to make better educated predictions on who may perform well - it may also enrich their experience as they get to know players more. Currently, there are a few fantasy football stat tracking aides, however, they generally are behind paywalls and are not as user-friendly. I believe there is room for improvement in this niche - there is room to improve the fantasy football experience. Creating a simple stat tracker allows enthusiasts and casual players to learn more of a player they intend to draft/pick up from waivers.

Proposed Application

With a couple years of experience in being an active member of the fantasy football community, I believe an application or webpage that consolidates and displays information of players would be beneficial for a quick-glance type of resource. By creating an info-summary of players based on their past season, fantasy football managers can quickly access the performance of a certain player and gleam some insight from their statistics. There are a couple resources that attempt to do this, for example - FantasyPros, but they are often behind a paywall and typically not beginner friendly. I will be drawing from a dataset that provides the relevant statistics for fantasy football. Using this data, I intend to allow users to display player stat summaries by position - this should a allow for more specificity in filtering through player data.
